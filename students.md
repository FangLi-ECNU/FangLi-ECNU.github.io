## Available Positions

### M.Sc. Positions

We are constantly seeking students to join our research team as Master of Science (M.Sc.) students. Students should be highly self-motivated and innovative, with strong interest in image processing, computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning.

**TL;DR**: *The most decisive quality we want to see from you is your enthusiasm.*

**Programming** (Required):
- Solid programming skills are required — however, you don't need to be an expert.
- Languages used among us: Matlab (default), Python, Julia
- For deep learning: familiarity with Python and Linux is required. PyTorch is our default framework.

**Mathematics** (Required):
- Linear algebra, mathematical modeling, optimization algorithms, and numerical methods
- Bonus: probability & statistics, functional analysis, differential equations, differential geometry

**Languages** (Required):
- You must read and write extensively in English — all up-to-date work is in English.
- Fluent spoken English is an advantage.

**Others** (Optional):
- Related background is not strictly required but is a great advantage.
- Tools like LaTeX, GitHub, Linux, command-line are what you will meet daily.

FAQs for potential M.Sc. students:
- You are encouraged (and will be pushed) to write your own papers ASAP.
- Courses are mainly in seminar format — I supervise and guide, not lecture.
- Be self-motivated — don't wait for me to reach you.
- You're free to choose any relevant field you're interested in.

### Ph.D. Positions

Regularly, there is one position available per year for potential Ph.D. students. Please reach me as soon as possible if you're interested.
